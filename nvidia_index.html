What is the NVIDIA AI-Powered Smart Edge Platform?

It's an AI-driven IoT solution that processes real-time data at the edge using NVIDIA's AI technology, providing instant insights and automation.

How can I integrate my IoT devices with this solution?

Our platform supports a wide range of IoT devices and provides APIs, SDKs, and plug-and-play integrations for easy setup.

No, it processes data on-site with NVIDIA's AI edge technology, reducing latency and enhancing security. Cloud storage is optional for historical data.

Smart cities, healthcare, industrial automation, retail, logistics, and more. Any industry needing real-time AI insights from IoT devices can benefit.
